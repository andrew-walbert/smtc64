Member Network Agent

SC64 is a private AI professional network manager, not another job-search dashboard.

The SC64 Member Network Agent is not just a career or job-search agent. It is a private AI-powered professional network manager that helps members stay connected to the companies, people, roles, tools, groups, recruiters, and warm paths where mutual value is most likely.

Search Modes

Private Pool Search vs. Open Market Expansion.

Private Pool Search

The premium members-only layer searches SC64-reviewed, member-approved, human-filtered records first.

  • approved companies, people, recruiters, and trusted contacts
  • warm paths and prior relationship intelligence
  • member-owned target universes and private notes
  • recommended before any broader search

Open Market Expansion

Broader public or external sources are used only when needed, and results stay outside the trusted pool until reviewed.

  • marked AI-suggested or needs verification
  • reviewed before being trusted
  • never treated as approval to contact
  • used to expand, not replace, the private layer

How Membership Works

Private, structured, and built around small high-signal actions.

Summit Club 64 is not a job board, resume shop, or traditional recruiting agency. It is a private professional network system for leaders whose current performance and future opportunity both depend on network strength.
