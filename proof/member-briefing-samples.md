# Member Briefing Samples

These are public-safe examples of the style of private member briefings SC64 could support.

They are illustrative only and do not expose private member data.

## Weekly Network Brief

**Theme:** GTM leadership market activity

**Signal:** Several approved target companies show recent hiring movement around revenue leadership, sales operations, and enterprise account teams.

**Recommended actions:**

- Review three target companies for leadership changes.
- Reconnect with one trusted recruiter covering enterprise software.
- Save two relevant roles for market calibration, not immediate application.
- Draft one low-pressure note to a former peer.

## Group Signal Filter

**Theme:** Sales leadership group activity

Most recent posts were low-signal. One tool discussion is worth reviewing because multiple credible GTM operators engaged with it and the category overlaps with approved target companies.

**Recommended action:** Save the tool and watch whether target companies mention it again before engaging.

## Warm Path Alert

**Theme:** Potential relationship path

A trusted second-degree path appears connected to a target company hiring in a relevant function.

**Recommended action:** Draft a short, non-urgent reconnection note. Do not ask for a referral until the relationship context is stronger.

## Recruiter Movement

**Theme:** Search coverage change

A recruiter connected to the member's target market appears to have moved into a more relevant company or search lane.

**Recommended action:** Update the recruiter map and prepare a short note tied to market fit, not job-seeking urgency.

## Permission Reminder

The agent may monitor, recommend, and draft. It should not send, post, connect, or disclose private member context without explicit approval.
