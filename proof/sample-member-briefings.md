# Sample Member Briefings

These examples are public-safe and illustrative. They do not expose private member data.

## Weekly Network Brief

**Signal:** Two approved companies show quiet GTM hiring movement. One trusted recruiter changed roles. Three peer contacts are worth a low-pressure reconnect.

**Recommended action:** Review the ranked queue. Draft messages only. Do not send until approved.

## Telegram-Style Brief

> I reviewed your approved company list and trusted groups. Most updates were not worth interrupting you. One target company added a revenue operations leader, and two warm paths may now be relevant. I recommend holding public action and reviewing the private queue.

## Discreet Reconnect Brief

**Recommended contact:** former peer in enterprise software

**Why now:** visible role change and possible path into approved target company

**Suggested action:** soft reconnect, no job-search language

**Approval:** required before sending
