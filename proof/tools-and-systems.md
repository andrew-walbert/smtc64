# Tools And Systems

SC64 can support approved member workflows across tools and systems, but does not present itself as uncontrolled automation.

## Possible Inputs

- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- member-approved company lists
- member-approved people lists
- trusted groups
- CRM records
- Airtable
- Google Sheets
- contact exports
- public market signals

## Possible Workflow Tools

- Phantombuster or similar approved signal intake and workflow packaging tools
- Telegram
- Slack
- email
- Airtable
- Google Sheets
- member-owned workflow nodes

## Governance

- private monitoring is allowed inside approved boundaries
- public action requires explicit approval
- automation should be governed, logged, and permissioned
- sensitive contacts require extra confirmation
- open-market records require review before entering the trusted pool
- SC64 does not position Phantombuster or any similar tool as uncontrolled LinkedIn automation
- the member owns execution and must approve public-facing action
