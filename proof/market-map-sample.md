# Market Map Sample

This sample shows the structure of a public-safe market map.

## Search Theme

GTM recruiting and talent intelligence roles in high-growth US technology companies.

## Company Categories

- AI platforms and AI infrastructure
- cloud and data platforms
- cybersecurity and identity
- enterprise SaaS
- developer tools
- revenue technology
- growth-stage startup ecosystems

## Target Role Clusters

- Senior GTM Recruiter
- Senior Sales Recruiter
- Senior Sourcer, GTM / Sales
- GTM Talent Partner
- Talent Intelligence Partner
- AI Recruiting Operations Specialist
- Recruiting Workflow Consultant

## Benchmark Signals

Strong benchmark profiles usually show:

- GTM or sales recruiting depth
- hiring-manager partnership
- market mapping ability
- enterprise technology context
- sourcing systems discipline
- relationship-centered outreach
- clear proof without overstatement

## Output Format

| Company | Category | Relevant Personas | Why It Matters | Next Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example AI platform | AI / enterprise software | GTM recruiter, sales recruiter, talent partner | Strong fit for AI + GTM recruiting positioning | Review current talent team and open roles |
| Example cybersecurity company | Security / enterprise sales | sales recruiting leader, GTM sourcer | Aligns with enterprise sales and systems engineering hiring | Map benchmark profiles |
| Example data platform | Cloud / data | GTM talent partner, sourcer | Strong relevance for SaaS, data, and enterprise motion | Identify warm paths |

## Public-Safe Boundary

This sample does not expose private contacts, proprietary search data, or confidential company intelligence.
