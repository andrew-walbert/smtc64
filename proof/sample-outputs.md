# Sample Outputs

These examples show the type of public-safe output the Andrew AI Career Agent can produce.

## GTM Talent Map

**Target role:** Senior Enterprise Account Executive

**Likely feeder companies:** enterprise SaaS, cloud, cybersecurity, data infrastructure, identity, observability, and AI platform companies.

**Target signals:**

- enterprise or strategic account ownership
- complex deal cycles
- technical buyer environment
- vertical or named-account selling
- strong manager or peer references
- product category relevance

**Next action:** build a 40-company map, identify 3-5 current title variants, and compare 10 benchmark profiles before outreach.

## Target Persona

**Persona:** GTM talent partner for AI or enterprise SaaS company.

**Relevant signals:**

- sales or GTM recruiting scope
- hiring-manager partnership
- sourcing strategy ownership
- market mapping experience
- ATS/CRM workflow discipline
- comfort with AI-assisted research and documentation

## Outreach Angle

**Context:** reconnecting with a GTM leader in a relevant company.

**Angle:** mention a specific GTM hiring or market signal, keep the ask light, and offer a useful perspective rather than asking for a job.

**Draft style:** short, grounded, and easy to ignore or answer.

## Recruiter Relationship Plan

- identify recruiters covering GTM, sales, enterprise software, AI, and talent intelligence roles
- group them by company, search firm, and relationship strength
- prioritize 5 warm or relevant recruiters per week
- send short, specific updates tied to target role fit
- track follow-up timing and signal quality

## LinkedIn Audit

Focus areas:

- headline clarity
- About section proof
- GTM and enterprise sales recruiting keywords
- AI workflow credibility
- target company relevance
- Featured section assets
- profile-to-portfolio connection

## Weekly Network Action Brief

Example structure:

- 3 profile or portfolio actions
- 3 relationship actions
- 2 company intelligence actions
- 2 opportunity actions

The goal is a small, repeatable operating rhythm rather than a large public campaign.
