# Sample Telegram Member Briefing

This is an example of how the SC64 Member Network Agent could feel inside Telegram, Slack, email, or another preferred member channel.

## Member Question

> What is happening in my network that matters?

## Agent Briefing

Three items are worth your attention this week.

1. Two approved companies show GTM hiring movement. One opened a senior sales leadership role. The other added three enterprise sales roles and appears to be building a new regional team.
2. One trusted contact changed roles and is now closer to a company in your approved universe. This is a good soft-reconnect candidate.
3. A sales tool appeared in recent group activity. Most posts were low signal, but three trusted GTM leaders engaged with this one and two approved companies appear to be using it.

Recommended action:

- Reconnect with the role-change contact.
- Watch the two hiring companies for one more week before outreach.
- Save the sales tool to the approved watch list.

Draft-only option:

> Saw your move and wanted to say congrats. Looks like a strong fit from the outside. Would be good to catch up when timing is easy.

I will not send anything unless you approve.
