# Sample Network Activation Plan

This example shows what can happen when a member says:

> Activate my approved network.

## Agent Process

1. Search approved target companies
2. Identify likely hiring stakeholders
3. Rank contacts by relevance, relationship path, and trust level
4. Flag sensitive contacts
5. Draft message options
6. Prepare a review queue
7. Recommend delivery method: Telegram briefing, Airtable, Google Sheet, contacts export, or workflow package
8. Require approval before outreach

## Example Output

> I found 200 relevant hiring stakeholders across your approved company universe. I recommend contacting the top 25 first, routing 18 through warm introductions, holding 12 due to sensitivity, and reviewing 42 before adding them to the trusted pool. I drafted three outreach versions and will not send anything until you approve.

## Delivery Options

- Telegram briefing
- Slack briefing
- email summary
- Airtable queue
- Google Sheet
- contacts export
- workflow package
