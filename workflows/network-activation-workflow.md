# Network Activation Workflow

This workflow is used when a member says:

> Activate my approved network.

It is designed for moments when a member loses a job, wants to quietly explore a substantial opportunity, or needs to understand the strongest warm paths available without publicly signaling a search.

## Inputs

- approved company universe
- approved roles and functions
- approved geography or market scope
- trusted contacts and warm paths
- sensitive contacts or hold-back list
- member voice and outreach preferences
- delivery preference: Telegram, Slack, email, Airtable, Google Sheet, contact export, or workflow package

## Process

1. Search approved target companies first.
2. Identify likely hiring stakeholders and relevant relationship paths.
3. Rank contacts by relevance, trust level, relationship path, and timing.
4. Flag sensitive contacts, conflicts, or reputationally risky paths.
5. Draft message options in the member's voice.
6. Prepare a review queue.
7. Recommend the best delivery method.
8. Require approval before any outreach or public action.

## Output

- ranked stakeholder list
- warm introduction paths
- sensitive-contact hold list
- review-needed records
- outreach drafts
- recommended first wave
- member approval checkpoint

## Example

> I found 200 relevant hiring stakeholders across your approved company universe. I recommend contacting the top 25 first, routing 18 through warm introductions, holding 12 due to sensitivity, and reviewing 42 before adding them to the trusted pool. I drafted three outreach versions and will not send anything until you approve.
