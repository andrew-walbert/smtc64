# Discreet Network Monitoring Workflow

Members may want to understand their market without publicly looking active.

The agent acts as a discreet professional sensing layer.

## What To Monitor

- approved companies
- approved contacts
- trusted groups
- recruiter movement
- hiring signals
- leadership changes
- relevant tools and communities
- peer network signals

## What To Avoid

- public behavior that signals a job search
- overreacting to weak signals
- sensitive outreach without confirmation
- noisy alerts
- forced outcomes

## Example

If a member joins a group like LinkedIn Tech Sales Stars, the agent monitors the noise, reviews tools/posts/signals, and only sends what is actually worth the member's time.

Example briefing:

> I reviewed recent activity from your approved sales leadership groups. Most of it was noise. One new sales tool is relevant because three trusted GTM leaders in your network engaged with it and two target companies appear to be using it.
