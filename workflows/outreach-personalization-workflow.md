# Outreach Personalization Workflow

This workflow supports thoughtful, relationship-centered outreach.

## Principles

- relevance over volume
- clarity over cleverness
- no false familiarity
- no fake urgency
- no spam automation
- one clear reason for reaching out
- one simple next step

## Inputs

- target person
- company context
- role or relationship reason
- shared background or credible relevance
- useful public signal
- desired next action

## AI-Assisted Drafting

AI can help draft:

- connection request options
- recruiter check-in notes
- hiring manager outreach
- founder/operator intros
- follow-up messages
- short comment ideas

## Human Review

Every message should be reviewed for:

- accuracy
- tone
- specificity
- privacy
- relationship fit
- whether the message would still feel acceptable if forwarded

## Output

The output should be a short draft and a reason:

- message draft
- why this angle fits
- what not to say
- follow-up timing
