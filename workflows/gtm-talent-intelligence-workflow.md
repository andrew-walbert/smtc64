# GTM Talent Intelligence Workflow

This workflow shows how Andrew structures a GTM talent intelligence project.

## 1. Define The Hiring Context

- role family
- reporting line
- territory or market
- stage of company
- sales motion or GTM model
- must-have experience
- nice-to-have signals
- compensation or level assumptions when available

## 2. Build Target Personas

The workflow translates the role into searchable talent personas:

- current titles
- prior titles
- adjacent functions
- company types
- deal sizes or customer segments
- product or technical context
- leadership scope
- likely keywords

## 3. Map Companies

Target companies are grouped by relevance:

- direct competitors
- category peers
- customer-adjacent companies
- high-growth SaaS and AI companies
- enterprise technology companies
- alumni-rich companies
- likely feeder companies

## 4. Organize Relationship Paths

The output should identify possible paths into the market:

- first-degree relationships
- second-degree paths
- alumni paths
- recruiter/search-firm paths
- investor/founder/operator paths
- relevant groups or communities

## 5. Produce Actionable Output

The final output should be useful to a hiring manager, recruiter, founder, or operator:

- target company list
- target persona notes
- search strings or sourcing themes
- outreach angles
- relationship paths
- follow-up priorities
- assumptions to validate
