# Sourcing Intake Workflow

The sourcing intake workflow turns a vague hiring need into a focused sourcing plan.

## Intake Questions

- Why is this role open now?
- What business outcome does the hire support?
- What profiles have worked before?
- What profiles have failed?
- Which companies should be considered strong feeders?
- Which companies should be avoided?
- What experience is truly required?
- What can be learned on the job?
- What is the hiring manager willing to trade off?
- What does a successful first six months look like?

## AI-Assisted Preparation

AI can help before the intake by:

- summarizing public role requirements
- comparing similar titles
- identifying likely target-company categories
- generating calibration questions
- drafting candidate persona hypotheses
- preparing role-specific market map starters

## Human Judgment

The recruiter or talent intelligence operator still owns:

- calibration
- credibility
- relationship quality
- candidate experience
- hiring-manager alignment
- final search strategy

## Output

The intake should produce:

- role brief
- target persona
- company map
- keyword map
- outreach themes
- open risks
- next sourcing steps
