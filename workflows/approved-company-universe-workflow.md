# Approved Company Universe Workflow

The approved company universe is the member's private list of companies worth watching.

Example:

A member approves 100 companies. SC64 mirrors those companies into the Master Network Node and monitors them for hiring, leadership moves, tools, groups, people, recruiters, peer activity, and warm-path signals.

## Inputs

- approved company list
- target roles and functions
- excluded companies
- sensitive companies
- trusted groups and communities
- known contacts
- warm introduction paths
- preferred briefing channel

## Process

1. Confirm the member-approved company list.
2. Attach roles, functions, groups, tools, and people to each company where known.
3. Mark sensitive companies or contacts.
4. Search the trusted private pool first.
5. Monitor approved public signals only where appropriate.
6. Package high-signal updates for member review.
7. Keep AI-suggested open-market records outside the trusted pool until reviewed.

## Signals To Track

- hiring movement
- leadership changes
- recruiter movement
- GTM team growth
- sales tool adoption
- relevant group activity
- peer network signals
- warm introduction paths

## Guardrails

- Company monitoring does not equal permission to contact.
- Open-market records remain needs-review until approved.
- Sensitive companies and contacts require extra confirmation.
- No public action happens without explicit member approval.
