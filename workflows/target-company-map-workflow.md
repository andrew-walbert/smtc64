# Target Company Map Workflow

This workflow organizes companies around fit, relevance, and relationship paths.

## Inputs

- target role or function
- target industry or segment
- preferred company stage
- geography or remote preference
- existing network signals
- comparable profiles
- saved jobs or followed companies
- known exclusions

## Company Tiers

Companies can be grouped into tiers:

- Tier 1: closest fit and strongest strategic relevance
- Tier 2: strong fit, useful market signal, or realistic relationship path
- Tier 3: exploratory, adjacent, or useful for learning

## Scoring Criteria

Each company can be scored by:

- role relevance
- GTM or technical alignment
- growth signal
- hiring signal
- connection path
- benchmark profile quality
- geography or operating model fit
- credibility value

## Output

The output should include:

- priority company list
- why each company matters
- likely relevant personas
- possible relationship paths
- open research questions
- next action
