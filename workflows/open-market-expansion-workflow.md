# Open Market Expansion Workflow

Open Market Expansion is used only when the trusted private pool does not answer the member's question well enough.

## Sources

Broader public or external sources may be reviewed when appropriate, but outputs must be treated as unverified until reviewed.

## Labels

Open-market records should be marked:

- AI-suggested
- needs verification
- not yet trusted
- not approved for outreach

## Process

1. Search broader sources only after private pool review
2. Summarize why each result may matter
3. Flag uncertainty and source limitations
4. Prepare a review queue
5. Add to trusted pool only after approval

## Guardrail

Open Market Expansion does not authorize outreach, scraping, or automated engagement.
