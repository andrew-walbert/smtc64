# Interview Positioning Workflow

This workflow prepares Andrew's professional story for interviews and recruiter screens.

## Inputs

- target job description
- company context
- Andrew's resume evidence
- relevant proof points
- likely objections
- market positioning

## Positioning Steps

1. identify what the role values most
2. map Andrew's evidence to those needs
3. translate experience into the company's language
4. prepare concise and expanded answers
5. note risks or claims to avoid
6. define follow-up questions Andrew should ask

## Answer Types

The workflow can produce:

- recruiter screen summary
- "tell me about yourself" answer
- role-fit answer
- AI workflow explanation
- sourcing strategy answer
- hiring-manager partnership answer
- GTM market mapping example
- objection response

## Boundary

The workflow should not invent numbers, confidential details, employers, or outcomes.

If a proof point is not verified, the answer should either omit it or mark it as needing validation.
