# Member Network DNA

Every Member Network Agent has its own network DNA.

SC64 starts with a shared Master Network Node, but every Member Network Agent becomes unique through the member's own professional DNA: their experience, strengths, target companies, approved contacts, groups, relationship history, goals, and boundaries.

Two members may both care about enterprise sales leadership, but their agents will not recommend the same companies, people, groups, or actions. Each agent is shaped by the member's approved network map and designed to help that member become more visible, useful, and connected in the professional ecosystem where they create the most value.

## Inputs

- professional persona
- resume and work history
- strengths and superpower skills
- target roles
- target companies
- approved contacts
- groups and communities
- preferred discretion level
- goals and boundaries
- relationship history, when approved

## Output

The Member Network DNA model should produce:

- member-specific positioning
- approved company universe
- relevant role and persona map
- groups and communities worth watching
- network categories
- relationship action rules
- sensitive-contact guardrails
- preferred delivery channel
- outreach voice and approval requirements

## Guardrails

- No private contacts in public docs.
- No Sales Navigator exports in GitHub.
- No confidential relationship notes in public files.
- No public action without explicit member approval.
